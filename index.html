Your Questions Answered About AM/PM Menopause Capsules

What are AM/PM Menopause Capsules?

AM/PM Menopause Capsules are a specially formulated dietary supplement designed to support women going through menopause. The AM capsule helps boost energy and stabilize mood during the day, while the PM capsule is tailored to improve sleep quality and reduce night sweats and hot flashes.

How should I take these capsules?

For optimal results, take one AM capsule in the morning, preferably with your breakfast, to help manage daytime symptoms. Take one PM capsule in the evening, ideally before bedtime, to support a restful night's sleep.

Are there any side effects?

AM/PM Menopause Capsules are made with natural ingredients and are generally well-tolerated. However, as with any supplement, some individuals may experience mild side effects such as digestive upset. If you have any concerns, please consult with your healthcare provider.

How long does it take to see results?

Results can vary based on individual health conditions and body chemistry. However, most users report experiencing noticeable improvements in their symptoms within the first few weeks of regular use.

Can I take these capsules with other medications?

While AM/PM Menopause Capsules are safe to use with most medications, we recommend consulting with your healthcare provider before starting any new supplement, especially if you are taking prescription medications.

Are these capsules gluten-free?

Yes, our AM/PM Menopause Capsules are gluten-free, making them suitable for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease.

What should I do if I miss a dose?

If you miss a dose, simply continue with your next scheduled dose. There is no need to double up. Maintaining a consistent routine is key to achieving the best results.
